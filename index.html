Welcome to the Blupee Conservation Society!

Blupees are a rare, mystical creature living in Hyrule. Very little is known about them or their origins, and studying them is difficult due to their skittish nature. Unfortunately, many adventurers will strike blupees to take the ruppees that blupees are known to collect. While they are very resilient, the Blupee Conservation Society is working dilligently to put an end to the needless violence.

Did You Know This About Blupees?

Blupees are often found near the entrance of caves, leading adventurers to treasure!

Blupees are know to disguise themselves as Bubbulfrogs and will drop a rare gem when their true identity is discovered.

While often found alone, blupees have been know to live in large, hidden groups, often under the protective care of the mysterious Satori.

Blupees rarely drop more than 30 ruppees. You would be better off mining gems for a quick buck.
